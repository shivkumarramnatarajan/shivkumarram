Treating the Job Search as a Data Science Problem
Independent Research
Built a human-in-the-loop AI pipeline reconstructing 2,841 application-event records from email and platform exports, orchestrating five LLMs (Claude, Gemma, Grok, Codex, LLaMA) across an eight-version script architecture developed over nine days. Authored a full academic paper applying personal informatics and computational social science frameworks — key findings include a 64% application non-response rate and email accounting for 97% of interview-stage evidence.

Gen Z Perception on Marketing Strategies of UPI Service Providers
University Research Project · Christ University
Designed and executed a primary research study on how Indian UPI providers (PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm) market ancillary services to Gen Z in urban Bengaluru. Collected 202 valid responses and analysed data in IBM SPSS using ANOVA, T-tests, Chi-Square, and Pearson Correlation. Identified a significant positive correlation (r = 0.375, p < 0.001) between UPI adoption characteristics and uptake of cross-sold services. Awarded an excellent grade.
